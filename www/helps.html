Functions:

Click on empty space to create vertices(represented by a circle)

Click on a vertex to select it, it will become visually bigger.

Once a vertex is selected:

- Click on an empty space to move it.

- Click on it again to cancel.

- Click another vertex to add an edge, or if an edge already exist, remove the edge between them.



Options:

1. Type of colouring, done automatically after edge addition and change in selection.

2. Size of the circles representing vertices.
